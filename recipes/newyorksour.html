New York Sour Cocktail

Wine + Bourbon. Can't get any better than this.

Simple to execute, yet looks impressive and tastes complex

Ingredients

  • 2 oz of bourbon
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 12 oz red wine
  • Egg white (optional)

Steps

  1. Add bourbon, lemon juice, simply syrup, and egg white into a shaker with ice
  2. shake hard
  3. strain into a nice glass over some ice
  4. slowly pour red wine over the back of a spoon so it floats at the top of the drink
