Honey Buns

College student essential.

Hungry, broke, and short on time? Have a Honey Bun!

Ingredients

  • Honey Bun

Steps

  1. Walk into any stores or deli with confidence
  2. Grab a Honey Bun from one of the shelf
  3. Smile and pay at the cashier
  4. Crack it open from the open, and slowly push the bun out from the opening
  5. Indulge yourself in some of the finest pastries to ever grace this Earth, Enjoy!
